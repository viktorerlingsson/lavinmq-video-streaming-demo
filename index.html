How to Use

This demo streams video frames through LavinMQ using AMQP stream queues. Each frame is published as a message and consumed in real-time via WebSocket.

  1. Open Video Source below and click Publish Video to extract and publish frames from a sample video to LavinMQ. This only needs to be done once — frames persist in the stream queue.
  2. Press play to start playback. Use the transport controls to rewind, fast-forward, or skip to the start/end.
  3. Adjust Speed to change playback rate relative to the original FPS. Max drains frames as fast as possible.
  4. Adjust Size to change the video display resolution.
  5. Open Statistics to see consumer, connection, and video details.
